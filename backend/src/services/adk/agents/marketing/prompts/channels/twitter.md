# Twitter/X Platform Guidelines

## Character Limits
- Maximum: 280 characters per tweet
- Ideal: 100-200 characters for highest engagement
- Threads: Break longer content into 2-3 connected tweets

## Hashtag Strategy
- Use 1-2 highly relevant hashtags maximum
- Place at end of tweet or integrated naturally
- Research trending hashtags when relevant

## Best Practices
- Lead with a hook or question
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max)
- Include a call-to-action when appropriate
- Leave room for retweets with commentary

## Example Format
```
[Hook or question] [Value proposition] [Emoji] [#Hashtag]
```
