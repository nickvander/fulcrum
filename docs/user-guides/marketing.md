# Marketing User Guide

The Marketing module helps you grow your business by reaching customers across
multiple platforms.

## Overview Dashboard

When you open the Marketing page, you'll see:

- **KPI Widgets**: Key metrics like active campaigns, scheduled posts, and
  recent results.
- **Calendar View**: A visual schedule of your upcoming marketing activity.
- **Campaign List**: A detailed table of your ongoing and past campaigns.

## Creating a Campaign

1. Click the **New Campaign** button.
2. **Setup**: Provide a name, budget, and description.
3. **Products**: Select the products you want to promote.
4. **Schedule**: Set the dates for your campaign.
5. **Events**: (After creation) Click the **Calendar** to add specific posts or
   emails within that campaign window.

## Using Quick Posts

Quick Posts are perfect for immediate updates that don't need a full campaign.

1. Click **New Quick Post**.
2. Select your **Channel** (e.g., "Instagram Business" or "Store Newsletter").
3. Add your **Content** and a **Photo URL**.
4. Link any **Products** if you want to track sales from this post.
5. Click **Post Now** to publish immediately.

## AI Content Assistant

> **Prerequisite:** Enable AI in **Settings → AI & Agents**. See the
> [AI Integration Guide](/guides/ai-integration) for setup instructions.

The AI Content Assistant helps you create engaging social media content quickly.

### How to Use

1. Open **Quick Post** and link a product.
2. The **AI Content Assistant** panel appears (if AI is enabled).
3. **Select a Tone:** Click a chip (Professional, Casual, Viral, Luxury, or
   Custom).
4. **Edit the Prompt:** The prompt pre-fills based on your tone selection. You
   can customize it before generating.
5. Toggle **Generate Image** if you want AI-generated visuals.
6. Click **Generate** to create content.
7. Review and edit the generated text, then **Post Now** or **Save Draft**.

### Tone Presets

| Tone         | Best For                           |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| Professional | B2B, formal announcements          |
| Casual       | Friendly, conversational posts     |
| Viral / Hype | High-energy, engagement-focused    |
| Luxury       | Premium brands, aspirational       |
| Custom       | Write your own prompt from scratch |

### Saving Drafts

When you save a draft, AI metadata is preserved:

- The tone and prompt you used
- The model that generated the content
- Timestamp of generation

This helps you iterate on content or replicate successful posts later.

## Managing the Calendar

The calendar allows you to visualize your workflow and make changes on the fly.

- **Drag & Drop**: Click and hold a post to move it to a different day. The
  schedule will update automatically.
- **Weekly/Monthly Views**: Toggle between views using the buttons at the top of
  the calendar.
- **Detail View**: Click any event on the calendar to see its full content and
  status.

## Filtering Your View

Use the toolbar to focus on what matters:

- **Date Range**: View all time, just this week, or a custom range.
- **Status**: Filter by "Active", "Scheduled", or "Draft".
- **Channels**: Filter for specific connectors like "Email" or "Social".

## Best Practices

- **Connect Products**: Always link products to your posts. This allows Fulcrum
  to track conversions and suggest "Smart Boosts" for low-velocity items.
- **Consistent Schedule**: Aim for a balanced calendar. Avoid bunching all your
  posts into one day; spread them out for better engagement.
- **Draft Mode**: Save complex ideas as Drafts. You can always refine them later
  and schedule them once they're ready.
